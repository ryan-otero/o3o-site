TYPE: think
TITLE: Block Explorers Are Broken for Normal People
TAG: idea
DATE: March 16, 2026
BODY: Every block explorer assumes you already know what you're looking at. Raw hex, gas denominations, cryptic status codes — it's a dashboard built by engineers for engineers. There's a real product gap here: an AI-native explorer that abstracts the noise and explains what's actually happening in plain language. Tooltips, plain-English transaction summaries, contextual icons — the block explorer redesigned for humans first.
Smart contracts are the worst offender. You can verify a contract today, but reading the code is useless if you don't speak Solidity. An AI layer that breaks the contract into conceptual sections — here's what this part does, here's what triggers it, here's what it controls — would make verified contracts actually meaningful to the people using them.
