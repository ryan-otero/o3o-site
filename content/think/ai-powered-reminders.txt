TITLE: AI-Powered Reminders
TAG: idea
DATE: March 16, 2026
BODY:
Bookmarking is broken. Save something, never see it again. The problem isn't saving — it's that nothing ever surfaces it back to you.
The idea: a brain dump layer where you throw in anything — links, thoughts, half-formed ideas — and the AI handles the organizing. No manual tagging, no folders. Just input.
From there it reminds you on a cadence (daily, weekly) and over time starts classifying what you're actually saving. Patterns emerge. You don't have to think about structure upfront — it builds itself from usage.
